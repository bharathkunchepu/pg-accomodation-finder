Problem Statement

  • Students and professionals struggle to find suitable PG accommodations
  • Lack of centralized platform for PG listings
  • Difficulty in filtering by budget, location, and amenities
  • No easy way for owners to manage their listings
  • Limited access to reviews and ratings
Solution: A comprehensive web application that connects students with PG owners, providing search, filter, booking, and management capabilities.

  • CRUD operations update in-memory state
  • Can be extended to API backend

User Data

  • Reviews: localStorage (per PG)

  • Login status: localStorage
  • Persists across sessions
